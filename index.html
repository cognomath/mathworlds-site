Home

Math Worlds is a young nonprofit organization.
The mission of Math Worlds is to support every preschooler and elementary school student in
developing perseverance, problem solving skills, and a love of mathematics.
To achieve this, Math Worlds is developing a structured collection of online games and
activities that will let children learn mathematics in a highly individualized, efficient and enjoyable way.
What is special about MW games?

About Us

We aspire to develop Math Worlds: a new generation of online games and puzzles.
What makes Math Worlds game collection unique:

  • The games cover all but language component of comprehensive preK-5 curriculum plus introduction to algebra.
  • They will require minimal adult supervision and support.
  • While playing, children will develop new knowledge, not only practice skills.
  • Children will progress in their own style and pace.
