Learn Math Your Way

Math Worlds is a young nonprofit organization.

The mission of Math Worlds is to support every preschooler and elementary school student in developing
perseverance, problem-solving skills, and a love of mathematics.
To achieve this, Math Worlds is developing a structured collection of online games and activities that will let children learn mathematics
in a highly individualized, efficient and enjoyable way.

What is special about MW games?

We aspire to develop Math Worlds: a new generation of online games and puzzles.
What makes Math Worlds game collection unique:

  • The games cover all but language component of comprehensive preK-5 curriculum plus introduction to algebra.
  • They will require minimal adult supervision and support.
  • While playing, children will develop new knowledge, not only practice skills.
  • Children will progress in their own style and pace.

Nina Dubinsky has been teaching math to kids of all ages and also has experience in curriculum design and educational research.

Anna Shapiro-Mackler's experience ranges from software development to owning and running a printing shop,
from being an account manager for an offshore company to homeschooling a special needs child. She is an expert in problem-solving, whether the problems are in business or in math.

As high school students we both experienced the joy of learning math by problem-solving and exploration.
That's why we understood each other immediately when we discussed our vision for ideal online educational children's games.

We imagine children playing online games of their choice, enjoying the process,
and learning mathematics naturally when and where it is needed, just like they would learn a language.

Our dream is to let children experience the joy of playing and the excitement of learning mathematics - at the same time.

Math Worlds has been created to put this dream into practice.
