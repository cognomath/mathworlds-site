MATH WORLDS

Math Worlds is a young nonprofit organization.
The mission of Math Worlds is to support every preschooler and elementary school student in developing perseverance, problem solving skills, and a love of mathematics.
To achieve this, Math Worlds is developing a structured collection of online games and activities that will let children learn mathematics in a highly individualized, efficient and enjoyable way.
